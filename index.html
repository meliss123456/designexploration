Hongik Visual Communication Design

2019*

Design Exploration ! team 2: cotton-candy !








Every year, more and more tourists are coming to Korea from all over the world.
The Hongdae area is popular with tourists, but they don't seem to show
any interest in the thing that gave the area its name - Hongik University.
Koreans all know about Hongik University, and they associate the Hongdae area
with the university itself as a place of creativity and artistic expression.
But tourists don't see that side of things. They visit Ewha University, and they visit Yonsei University,
but no one seems interested in Hongik University. It's like we're invisible.
We don't know why this is. Is it an image problem? Is it the campus?
What can we do to raise our profile?
